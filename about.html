About Me

My name is Jeremy Neilson. I'm a UX Designer and an aspiring Front End Designer based out of Salt Lake City, Utah. I am married to a beautiful wife and have a rambunctious little boy that keeps me busy. I graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelors degree in Film and Media Arts with an animation emphasis. It was then I really decided I loved the design aspect of animation more than anything.

Once I decided that, I set out to become a web developer/designer. I started with UX Design by attending a DevMountain course and soon decided not only do I want to design the websites, I'd love to code them as well. My ideal job would be a UX Designer / Front End Developer, so that is what I've set my sights on. I love sports, video games, playing music, and hanging out with my little family, pretty much anything adventurous, count me in.
