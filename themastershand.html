The Project

For this school project we were given the task of doing a surrealistic picture. Surrealism combines a dream like state which can also be very realistic. I chose to do a picture entitled, The Master's Hand. It portrays a beautiful girl being controlled by her master. This project consisted of photographing multiple images, editing, and manipulating each one of them and then combining them to make one cohesive image.
