The Project

For this school project we had to choose one of our favorite bands and essentially "rebrand" them with a poster. We were asked to not use any of the bands actual logos but to come up with different ways to visually define them. This is what I came up with for Angels & Airwaves (AvA). Overall I'm very happy with the whole project and how it turned out. I came up with something I'd be proud of to hang up in my room and call my own.
